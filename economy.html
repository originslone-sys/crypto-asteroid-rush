FAIR GAME MECHANICS

Our economy is designed to reward skill while maintaining long-term sustainability:

6 Lives System

Start each mission with 6 lives. Collide with asteroids and you lose a life. Lose all 6 and your mission earnings are forfeit. This rewards careful, skilled play.

90/10 Mission Balance

90% of missions are normal difficulty. 10% are "hard mode" with faster asteroids - a necessary economic balance that skilled players can still profit from.

3-Minute Missions

Each mission lasts exactly 180 seconds. This creates consistent earning potential and prevents exploitation while keeping gameplay engaging.

5 Missions Per Hour

Players can complete up to 5 missions per hour. This prevents system abuse while allowing dedicated players to earn consistently.

Important

This is a skill-based game. Your earnings depend on how well you play. Better players earn more - there's no guaranteed income. Play responsibly and only with funds you can afford to lose.

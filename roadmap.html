IMPORTANT: OUR DEVELOPMENT APPROACH

This roadmap represents our strategic vision and development goals for Crypto Asteroid Rush. However, we want to be completely transparent about our approach:

Flexibility is Key: In the dynamic world of blockchain gaming and technology, we maintain the flexibility to adapt our roadmap as needed. Updates, features, and timelines may be accelerated, delayed, or reprioritized based on:

  • Community feedback and priorities
  • Technical requirements and discoveries
  • Market conditions and opportunities
  • Security considerations and audits
  • Resource availability and team capacity

Quality Over Speed: We will never compromise on security, stability, or user experience to meet arbitrary deadlines. Each update undergoes rigorous testing before deployment to ensure the best possible experience for our commanders.

Community-Driven Development: Your feedback shapes our journey. We regularly consult with our community through Telegram, surveys, and gameplay data to ensure we're building features that truly matter to our players.

Stay tuned to our official Telegram channel for real-time updates and announcements!

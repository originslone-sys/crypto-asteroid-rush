PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Crypto Asteroid Rush ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate the Service:

  • Wallet Address: Your public blockchain wallet address when you connect
  • Game Data: Scores, earnings, and gameplay statistics
  • Transaction Records: Deposits, withdrawals, and stakes
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device info, IP address for security

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We specifically do NOT collect:

  • Your wallet private keys or seed phrases
  • Personal identification (name, email, phone)
  • Financial information beyond blockchain transactions

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Process your transactions and earnings
  • Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • Improve user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

5. Blockchain Transparency

Blockchain transactions are publicly visible. Your wallet address and transactions on BSC can be viewed by anyone on block explorers. This is inherent to blockchain technology.

6. Data Security

We implement security measures including encryption and secure servers. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

We use local storage to remember your wallet connection and preferences. You can clear this at any time.

8. Third-Party Services

We interact with MetaMask and Binance Smart Chain, which have their own privacy policies.

9. Data Retention

We retain data while your account is active. Blockchain records are permanent. Contact us via Telegram to request deletion of off-chain data.

10. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

11. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data, and to disconnect your wallet at any time.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.

13. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us through our Telegram community.

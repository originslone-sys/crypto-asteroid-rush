FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about Crypto Asteroid Rush.

General

What is Crypto Asteroid Rush?
Crypto Asteroid Rush is a play-to-earn arcade game where you pilot a spaceship to destroy asteroids and earn USDT cryptocurrency. Each valuable asteroid you destroy adds real money to your wallet. The game runs on Binance Smart Chain (BSC) for fast, low-cost transactions.
How does the game work?
1. Connect your MetaMask wallet on BSC network
2. Select one of 7 unique spaceships
3. Pay a small mission fee (0.00001 BNB)
4. Watch a brief loading screen
5. Play for 180 seconds - destroy asteroids and avoid collisions
6. Your USDT earnings are credited to your wallet automatically!
Is this game free to play?
Each mission costs 0.00001 BNB (less than $0.01). This small fee helps maintain game servers, prevents bot abuse, and ensures fair play for all users. You'll also need a tiny amount of BNB for blockchain gas fees.

What is the lives system?
Each mission starts with 6 lives. If an asteroid collides with your ship, you lose one life. After being hit, you get a brief moment of invincibility.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: If you lose ALL 6 lives before the mission ends, it's GAME OVER and you lose ALL your earnings from that mission! Survival is just as important as destroying asteroids.
What happens if I lose all my lives?
If you lose all 6 lives, the mission ends immediately as GAME OVER. You lose ALL earnings accumulated during that mission. The mission fee is not refunded. This makes the game skill-based - you need to balance offense (destroying asteroids) with defense (avoiding collisions).

How long is each mission?
Each mission lasts exactly 180 seconds (3 minutes). If you survive the full duration with at least 1 life remaining, you keep all your earnings. There's also a brief loading screen before the mission starts.
Is there a limit on how many missions I can play?
Yes, you can play up to 5 missions per hour. This limit helps ensure fair play and system stability.

Important: You can only have one active mission at a time. Complete your current mission before starting a new one.

We don't charge withdrawal fees. However, blockchain network fees (gas) may apply depending on network conditions. These are typically very low on BSC.

Staking

What is staking?
Staking allows you to earn passive income on your USDT balance. Instead of just holding your earnings, you can stake them to earn 12% APY (Annual Percentage Yield) with hourly compounding. Your staked funds continue to grow automatically!
What is the staking APY?
We offer 12% APY with hourly compounding. This means your earnings are calculated and added to your stake every hour, so you earn interest on your interest!
Is there a lock period for staking?
No! Our staking is completely flexible. You can unstake and withdraw your funds at any time with no penalties or waiting periods.
What is the minimum stake amount?
The minimum stake amount is $0.0001 USDT. You can stake any amount above this threshold.

Which blockchain does this use?
We use Binance Smart Chain (BSC) for fast and low-cost transactions. Make sure your MetaMask is configured for BSC (Chain ID: 56). The game will prompt you to switch networks if needed.
What wallet do I need?
You need MetaMask wallet. You can install it as a browser extension (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Brave) or use the MetaMask mobile app. Download from metamask.io
Can I play on mobile?
Yes! The game is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices. For the best experience, use the MetaMask mobile app's built-in browser to access the game.

Game Objective

Your mission is to destroy as many valuable asteroids as possible within 180 seconds while keeping your ship alive. Each asteroid you destroy earns you USDT cryptocurrency that goes directly to your wallet!

Key Points

  • Destroy asteroids to earn USDT rewards
  • Avoid collisions - you have 6 lives per mission
  • Target rare asteroids - they glow and are worth more!
  • Survive until the end - if you lose all lives, you lose all earnings!

Lives System

Each mission starts with 6 lives. If an asteroid collides with your ship, you lose one life. Be careful!

Game Over Warning

If you lose all 6 lives before the mission ends, it's GAME OVER and you lose ALL earnings from that mission! Your skill in dodging asteroids is just as important as destroying them.

Mission Difficulty

Missions have varying difficulty levels that affect asteroid speed and spawn rates.

Normal Mode (90% of missions)

Standard asteroid speeds and spawn rates. Your skill determines your earnings. Good players can consistently earn $0.02-0.05 per mission.

Hard Mode (10% of missions)

Faster asteroids, more frequent spawns, and more valuable asteroids. These missions are challenging! Focus on survival and dodge when needed.

Earnings Potential

Your earnings depend entirely on your skill! Here's what to expect:

  • Beginner: $0.01 - $0.02 per mission
  • Intermediate: $0.02 - $0.03 per mission
  • Expert: $0.03 - $0.05+ per mission

Remember: losing all lives means losing ALL earnings from that mission. Survival is key!

Pro Tips

  • Prioritize glowing asteroids - They're worth much more than common ones
  • Keep moving - A moving target is harder to hit
  • Don't chase common asteroids - They're worth $0.00, focus on survival
  • Watch the edges - Asteroids can appear from the sides
  • Hold FIRE - Continuous shooting is better than tapping
  • Learn asteroid patterns - They have slight horizontal drift
  • Use staking - Put your earnings to work with 12% APY
  • Play daily - Consistent players earn more over time

Mission Limits

To ensure fair play and system stability, there are some limits:

  • 5 missions per hour - Plan your sessions accordingly
  • 1 mission at a time - Complete your current mission before starting another
  • 3 minutes per mission - Each mission lasts exactly 180 seconds

Earnings Potential

With 5 missions per hour at an average of $0.02-0.03 each, skilled players can earn approximately $0.10-0.15 per hour. Focus on survival and valuable asteroids to maximize your earnings!

PRO TIPS

Strategies to maximize your earnings

Prioritize Rare Asteroids

Blue glowing asteroids are worth 3x more. Always aim for them first when they appear on screen.

Protect Your Lives

Losing all 6 lives means losing all earnings. Sometimes dodging is better than risking a collision.

Watch for Golden Glow

Legendary asteroids have a golden glow and are worth 20x a common. Never miss them!

Stay Mobile

Keep moving to avoid collisions. A stationary ship is an easy target for incoming asteroids.

Use Auto-Fire

Hold the fire button for continuous shooting. This helps maximize asteroid destruction.

Hard Mode Awareness

10% of missions are harder with faster asteroids. Stay alert and play more defensively.

Mission Limit

You can play up to 5 missions per hour. Plan your sessions wisely to maximize your earnings!
